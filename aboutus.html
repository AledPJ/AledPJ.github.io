2007 - 2010

The band was formed in 2007 after Brian Sella finished college and joined his childhood friend Mathew Uychich playing under The Front Bottoms. They were soon joined by Mathews brother Brian Uychich who began on the keyboard. The band released three EPs and two albums in this time as well as a 5 song cassete.


2010 - Present

It was around this time that the band began working on projects that would go on to become their new self-titled album. In 2010 Brian Uychich left the band to focus on school and the Front Bottoms replaced him with Drew Villafuerte until 2012 when he was replaced with Tom Warren. The band released albums and 5 EPs in this time.
